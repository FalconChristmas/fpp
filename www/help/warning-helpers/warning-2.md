# Log Level Set to Excessive
One of your logging settings is at the **Excessive** level. This logs a very large amount of detail, which can slow playback and fills the SD card quickly — a full SD card can corrupt or take FPP offline.

1. **Excessive** logging is meant only for short-term troubleshooting with a developer's guidance.
2. For normal show running, set the log level back to **Warn** or **Info** on the [Logging settings](../../settings.php#settings-logs) page.
3. After lowering the level, check free disk space (Status page) — clear old logs if the card filled up.
